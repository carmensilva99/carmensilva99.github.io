Who We Are

At SSW Thrift, we are dedicated to meeting your fashionable and affordable thrifting needs. We have a unique collection of gently used items including the latest fashions in brand name. Even if you are unsure of what you want, our pleasant staff will be happy to help you find exactly what you are looking for.

Created in 2020, by Carmen (S)ilva, Courtney (S)tewman, and Esther (W)on, we have managed to build a strong reputation in and around the vibrant city that is Chapel Hill.

If you have any questions or if you would like to check on the availability of an item, you can visit us at our address below, or call us at 919-555-5555. Hope to see you soon!
